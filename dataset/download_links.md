## noisedata/EnvironmentalBackgroundNoise
Data:
- Environmental background noise recordings used as part of the training noise pool.

Download:
- https://personal.utdallas.edu/~kehtar/NoiseData.rar

## noisedata/Noise15
Data:
- Research noise set with raw noise files such as `n101.raw`.

Download:
- https://github.com/speechdnn/Noises

## noisedata/NoiseX-92
Data:
- NoiseX-92 noise bank used for validation, testing, and generated noisy mixtures.

Download:
- https://github.com/speechdnn/Noises

## noisedata/Nonspeech
Data:
- Nonspeech noise clips used as an additional noise source.

Download:
- https://github.com/speechdnn/Noises
